Privacy Policy for our search engine and Go! Wiki

Introduction

We understand the importance of protecting your privacy online. That's why we've developed this privacy policy to inform you about the information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. It applies to all services offered by our search engine and Go!Wiki.

Information Collected

We do not collect any personal information from our users. We do not track users and we do not record the history of their searches. Search results are provided by Google, but we do not collect or store any personal data from our users. The information collected is automatically deleted.

How we use the information

We do not use the information collected to provide you with more relevant search results. We do not use this information for marketing purposes or to send you targeted advertisements.

Sharing of information

We do not share the information collected with third parties. We do not sell or rent any of your information to third parties.

Security of information

We take security measures to protect your information from loss, misuse and alteration.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us via email at gosearch@gosearch.ga or on Twitter by tweeting with the mention @gosearchoff.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this site.
